Under the Hood

Game Systems

⚔️ Active Combat
E to swing (canvas rotation arc, swing trail, flash). R to block. Enemy AI with patrol, chase, and attack states. Equip gear for higher max hit. Block reduces damage 50–75%.
🎣 Fishing
Bait rod at Draynor willows. Shrimp (10xp), Anchovies (40xp), Trout (50xp), Salmon (70xp). Failure chance, bait consumed per cast.
🪓 Woodcutting
Normal (25xp), Oak (37.5, Lv15), Willow (67.5, Lv30), Yew (175, Lv60). Trees fall randomly, stumps respawn. Failure at low levels.
⛏️ Mining
Clay (5xp) through Mithril (80xp). Rock depletion and respawn timers. 10 rocks in the Champion's mine, tiered by level requirement.
🔨 Smithing
Smelt bars at furnace (Bronze→Mithril). Smith weapons and armour at anvil. Full recipe UI with live material checks.
🏹 Fletching
Cut / String / Arrows tabs. Uses knife on logs for shafts and unstrung bows, strings bows, assembles arrows ×15. RS-accurate XP.
🍳 Cooking & 🔥 Firemaking
Cook raw fish, chicken, beef on fire or range. Burn chance scales with level. Light fires from inventory with tinderbox. Normal=40xp to Yew=202.5xp.
🛡️ Equipment & Gear
7 equipment slots. Click inventory items to equip, click gear slots to unequip. Attack and defence bonuses update live in the equipment panel.
🏦 Bank & 🏪 Shop
216 bank slots (6 pages), shared Lumbridge/Draynor. General Store with 19 items, dynamic pricing, buy/sell tabs, and gradual restock.
📜 Quests & NPCs
Cook's Assistant auto-tracks and completes. Imp Catcher collects 4 coloured beads. 8 quests in the log. Every NPC has full typed dialogue.
